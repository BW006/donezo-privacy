Donezo Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Donezo mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Authentication Data

We offer multiple authentication methods:

  1. Email/Password Registration:
    • Email address
    • Encrypted password
    • Account creation date
  2. Sign in with Apple:
    • Apple provided user identifier
    • Email address (if authorized by user)
    • Name (if authorized by user)
    • Account creation date
  3. Google Sign-In:
    • Google provided user identifier
    • Email address
    • Basic profile information
    • Account creation date

Personal Information

When you use the App, we collect:

  • Basic profile information
  • Habit tracking data (including habit names, descriptions, progress, and completion status)
  • App usage statistics
  • Subscription status and purchase history

Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App interaction data (features used, time spent)
  • Performance data (crash reports, diagnostics)
  • Analytics data (anonymized usage patterns)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your account
  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Process your subscription
  • Send you important updates and notifications
  • Provide customer support
  • Analyze app performance and usage patterns
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

Data Storage and Security

Authentication Security

  • Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Third-party authentication tokens are securely stored
  • All authentication data is encrypted in transit and at rest

Firebase

  • We use Firebase for authentication and data storage
  • Analytics data is anonymized
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We follow Firebase's security best practices

Apple Services

  • Sign in with Apple authentication
  • App Store for payments and subscription management
  • iCloud for data sync
  • StoreKit for in-app purchases

Google Services

  • Google Sign-In authentication
  • Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore
  • Google Analytics for Firebase (anonymized)

Third-Party Services

Authentication Providers

  • Apple (Sign in with Apple)
  • Google (Google Sign-In)
  • Firebase Authentication

Each provider's privacy policy applies to their respective authentication services:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy

OpenAI Integration

  • AI features use OpenAI's API
  • Habit suggestions are generated using anonymized data
  • No personal information is shared with OpenAI
  • AI processing follows OpenAI's privacy guidelines

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of data collection
  • Export your data
  • Cancel your subscription

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Required by law
  • Needed for legitimate business purposes

When you delete your account:

  • Personal information is removed within 30 days
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained
  • Backup data is removed within 90 days

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Sending you a notification
  • Requiring re-acceptance if changes are material

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). These rights include:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect
  • The right to delete your personal information
  • The right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information (note: we do not sell your information)
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights
